Alaska Html Mail Demo

 

Usage:

 

Email:

Enter your e-mail address. The mail will be sent to this

account.

 

Server:

Enter the name of your mail server (example:

mail.nowhere.com).

 

Username:

If your mailserver requires authentification, enter your

username.

 

Password:

Enter your password if required.

 

For sending the e-mail push button “Send mail”. The

SMTP log will be displayd In the XbpListBox and the mail

sent will be displayed in this XbpHtmlViewer.

 

The sample can also be invoked from command-line.

In this case email, server, username and password can be

passed as command-line parameters where username and

password are optional.

 

Have fun!
